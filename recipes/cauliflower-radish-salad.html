Cauliflower Radish Salad

Recipe source: Molded Memories Blog

This midcentury beauty practically jumped off the pages of Joys of Jell-O, a fun little booklet of jello recipes published by General Foods Corporation in 1967. I originally set out to find a recipe that actually sounds edible, but I can rarely resist the allure of an off-putting salad. I don’t want to live in a Cauliflower Radish Jello-free world knowing that such a thing exists.

Prep Time: To || Cook Time: Be || Servings: Determined

Ingredients:

  • 1 Package Lemon Jell-O
  • 1/2 tsp. Salt
  • 1 Cup Boiling Water
  • 3/4 Cup Cold Water
  • 1 Cup Diced Radishes
  • 3/4 Cup Chopped Raw Cauliflower
  • 4 tsp. Vinegar
  • 1 tsp. Grated Onion
  • (Optional) Taste buds

Preparation Steps:

  1. Combine the gelatin and salt, dissolve in boiling water, then stir in cold water.
  2. Chill until slightly thickened (about 20–30 minutes), using ice bath if needed.
  3. Mix cauliflower, radishes, vinegar, and grated onion; let marinate for 20 minutes.
  4. Fold vegetables into gelatin before it fully sets, pour into molds.
  5. Chill 4 hours or overnight. Unmold and serve on lettuce if desired.
